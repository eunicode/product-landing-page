Turtle Tab

Turtle Tab is a Chrome extension with a minimalist design and additional tools to help you be more productive.

Features

Turtle Tab features at a glance

  • Search Google from the homepage
  • See the local weather, or weather at any location
  • Write notes and todo lists
  • See your bookmarks
  • See and clear your history
  • Enable and disable Chrome extensions
